About

I am a hobbyist and a teacher by profession. After ditching social media for a long time in a bid to own my data, I developed this website to track and disseminate my knowledge and ideas to the world. This website will evolve as I devote time from my somewhat busy schedule to develop and integrate new features that will help me deliver expert content to my audience effectively.

Before settling on SvelteKit for the tech stack, I experimented with Hugo and blogdown but failed to keep up. It wasn't the fault of those stacks, but, to be honest, I had no motivation at that time to do the heavy lifting involved in maintaining a website. After learning data visualization for a year from scratch, the need of a website renewed strongly to showcase my projects. This time I chose GitHub Pages for hosting as I was fed up of spending money on commerical hosting.

The credit for developing logo of this site goes to maker.js which introduced to me the Google font Plaster and generated SVG of my initials free of cost. Earlier, I had used 5th Grade Cursive Font for the same purpose as it was identical to the initials in my signature.
